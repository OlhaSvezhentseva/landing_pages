Welcome to Picture Reference Game!

Please read the following information carefully before accepting the HIT. Clicking the button on the landing page will take you to the game.

Overview

  • Two players will see the same grids but in different orders.
  • Grids have empty cells and cells filled with "X".
  • One player will explain to the other one which grid is the target.
  • The other player will have to guess the target grid.
  • Each session includes 6 quick games. (~10 minutes).
  • You will not receive a payment, if you do not follow the rules!
  • If you play all the rounds and enter your token on Prolific, you will receive a payment.
  • If you win all the rounds, will get a 10% bonus. So try as hard as you can!
  • Participants who remain idle for 5 minutes or disconnect for more than 3 minutes, causing the session to end, will not be allowed to participate in further sessions and will not be payed.
  • A session will be shut down after the maximum allotted time, 5 minutes of idleness from a player or 5 minutes in the waiting room (in the event that no partner is found).

⚠️ NOTE: after completing the last round you will get a unique confirmation token.
Remember to copy it and paste it into Prolific in order to get your rewards. This is crucial. ⚠️
