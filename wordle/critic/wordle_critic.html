Welcome to Wordle Critic!

Please read the following information carefully before accepting the HIT. Clicking the button on the landing page will take you to a waiting room. Once another player arrives, you will both be moved to the task room where you can begin the task.

Overview

  • You will play a modified version of Wordle for 2 players.
  • One player (guesser) will have 6 attempts to guess an English 5-letter word.
  • The other player (critic) will give feedback on the choice of the guesser, before it is submitted, giving them an opportunity to change their guess.
  • There will be letter feedback for each submitted guess shown to both players.
  • Each session includes 3 words (~20 minutes).
  • Participants who finish the experiment will receive a payment.
  • Participants who remain idle for 5 minutes or disconnect for more than 3 minutes, causing the session to end, will not be allowed to participate in further sessions.
  • A session will be shut down after the maximum allotted time, 5 minutes of idleness from one player, or 5 minutes in the waiting room (in the event that no partner is found).

⚠️ NOTE: after completing the third word you will get a unique confirmation token.
Remember to copy it and paste it into Prolific in order to get your rewards. This is crucial. ⚠️
⚠️ Please disable any JavaScript blocking extension on your browser ⚠️
